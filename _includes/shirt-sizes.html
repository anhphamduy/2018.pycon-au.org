PyCon AU uses Gildan t-shirts, and this year's design will be available in a variety of colours that you can choose from when you collect your shirt. There are three varieties available:

  • The Straight-Cut option is the Ultra Cotton "Classic Fit" shirt
  • The Semi-Fitted option is the Heavy Cotton Semi-Fitted Ladies' shirt
  • The Classic Fitted option is the Ultra Cotton "Classic Fit" Ladies' shirt
    Please note that the Classic Fit cut may change. If it does, we will advise anyone who has selected this cut.
